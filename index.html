About Me

I'm an experienced digital marketer with a passion for people, creative ideas and the power of digital storytelling. A graduate of the University of Washington, I studied Communications and Cultural Anthropology while leading kayak tours in the summer. Today I can be found enjoying copious amounts of fine espresso while crafting search strategy with my team at Wheelhouse DMG.

When not perfecting my digital nomad skills, I'm likely pursuing adventures big and small. Whether snowboarding, traveling or seeking out the perfect sunset, I'm always on the hunt for beauty and joy. As a committed volunteer in my native Seattle, I have many years of experience supporting organizations close to my heart, like HRC, Daybreak Star and SJF, and more recently I've been named as a member to the GSBA's YPP council.
